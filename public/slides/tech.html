Core innovation
Two-phase engine for async human interaction
Phase 1 — runAIPhase()
AI agents discuss and pre-decide. Discussion events written to timeline. Decisions stored in session.result.pendingAIDecisions — hidden from timeline and UI. Status → deciding.
Human interaction
Human reads AI discussion + private reasoning. Makes split/steal decision via form. No AI sees the human's choice. Simultaneous commitment.
Phase 2 — submitHumanDecision()
All decisions revealed atomically. Human + AI decisions written to timeline. Payoffs calculated. Lightning settlement. Behavioral insight generated via LLM. Status → settled.
Lightning payment flow
Agent → human micropayment, end to end

02

03

04

Invoice created

LNbits createInvoice() for settlement record.

05

Human claims

Pastes bolt11 invoice. Arena calls payInvoice(). Sats move.
